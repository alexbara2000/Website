Birth

I was born February 9 in the year 2000. I was born in Romania Brasov where I ended up growing for the next 4 years of my life. I learned to speak Romanian and took my first steps in Romania. To this day I am grateful to be romanian since it thought me about the difficulties of life and how privileged we are to live in such a rich and beautiful country like Canada.

Family

In Romania is where my whole Family is located. To start off, I have my father Valentin Bara and my mother Adriana Bara. My parents raised me and I owe everything to them. The next part of my family is my brother Andrei Bara. We always fight but deep down we care for each other. I also have three cousins who I wish to see more often but cannot due to distance issues. I only have one grandmother left and 2 aunts. So my close family is relatively small.

School

During my time in Romania, I had time to go to only 2 years of pre school. Although I do not remember much of this time, my parents told me that I used to get along very well with the other kids and this has to do with my open personality. I used to be motivated and interested by touching and learning about evreything around me even at that young age.

Canada


When I was finally 4 years old, my parents decided to move to Canada in order to give me a better life. We moved here in montreal and have stayed here since. Every so often we go back to Romania to visit. The life conditions are a lot better here in Canada and I am very grateful for my parents moving here. It was a very hard time since I did not know the language and it was very hard to integrate in society.
